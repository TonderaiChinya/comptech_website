About Comptech

Comptech Engineering was founded in February 2011 by two brothers Tonderai and Clopas.

Since 2011, Comptech has been involved mostly in technical consultants and software support.
In this time we have successfully incoperated website design and development, and network systems.We are a mobile Cape Town entreprenuer company. We work with a variety of companies from startups to publicly traded corporations.

Our Mission

Our mission is to reach the goal of our clients, and provide service that best suits their needs.
By doing so, we treat every client's project with care. We strive for a modern website layout and/or modern network structures.
